London

London's story is one of continuous change, evolving from a Roman settlement to a vibrant global capital that has weathered centuries of conflict, plague, and redevelopment. The city boasts an ancient core, the City of London, that has served as a center for trade and commerce for over two millennia. Today, it remains a dynamic hub of finance, culture, and transportation, home to a diverse population speaking over 300 languages and a rich array of historic landmarks and modern innovations.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
