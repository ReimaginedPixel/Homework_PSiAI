Paris

Paris is France's capital, renowned for art, fashion, and gastronomy, nicknamed the "City of Light". Its history dates back to a Celtic tribe, the Parisii, around 259 BC, before becoming the Roman city Lutetia and later changing to Paris in the 4th century. The city is a global center of culture and finance, featuring iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and home to world-class museums such as the Louvre.

Paris became a major center of culture and business in Western Europe and is known as the "City of Light" (La Ville Lumière).
