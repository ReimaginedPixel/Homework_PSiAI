Tokyo

Tokyo's story is one of ancient origins as the fishing village of Edo, rising to become the powerful capital of the Tokugawa shogunate, and eventually the bustling, futuristic metropolis of Tokyo after the Meiji Restoration. It has endured devastating earthquakes and wars, transforming through rapid reconstruction into the vibrant, modern city it is today, a global hub for business, technology, and diverse culture.

Following World War II, Tokyo underwent rapid reconstruction, fueling Japan's economic boom and establishing it as a global economic powerhouse.
